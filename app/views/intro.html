The Gym Simulation

IF, in the morning,

  • the weights are perfectly racked - with each dumbell in a labelled slot that corresponds to the weight of the dumbell, and
  • EVERY attendee of the gym endevours to return his weights to the correct slot

THEN, oh what a perfect world it would be. However, as can be seen by running the simulations below in order, it only takes a few morons to ruin in for everyone. Like so many things in life ... :P

Simulatons Primer

In the simulations below, the following clarifications will help you understqnd what is going on:

  • the Green guys are "AverageJoes", they will put the dumbells in the correct slots if they are available. If the correct slot is not available, they will use the first available slot.
  • The Red guys are "Randoms", they put the weights back in random slots, with no regard for te correct placement. May they hurt their back while squatting!
